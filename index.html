About Us

Freetown Jazz Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality basketball training for players of all ages and skill levels. Our experienced coaches focus on developing fundamental skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship. AndThe Freetown Jazz Basketball Academy is a professional basketball team based in Sierra Leone Freetown Lumley. Founded in October 11 2019, we are members of the Western Area Basketball Association (WABBA) and play our home games at SOS School Lumley Beach Road Freetown.

Programs

Youth Development Program

Our Youth Development Program is designed to help young players develop their skills and passion for the game. We emphasize fundamentals, teamwork, and character development.

Elite Training Camps

Our Elite Training Camps offer advanced training for experienced players looking to take their game to the next level. Led by top coaches and players, these camps focus on advanced skills, strategy, and conditioning.

Skills Clinics

Our Skills Clinics provide focused training on specific aspects of the game, such as shooting, dribbling, defense, and conditioning. These clinics are open to players of all skill levels.

Summer Leagues

Our Summer Leagues offer competitive play in a fun and supportive environment. Players have the opportunity to compete against teams from across the region while developing their skills and teamwork.
